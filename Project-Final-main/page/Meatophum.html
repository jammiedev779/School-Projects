Overview
Meatophum serves 3 meals, all Cambodian traditional dishes, every day. You can find all type of Khmer traditional dishes, especially the rural ones, here including Prahok, Samlor Kako, Samlor Machu, Toek Kroeung, grilled fish, and many more.
