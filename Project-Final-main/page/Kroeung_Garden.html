Overview
The menu at Kroeung Garden Restaurant is inspired by Cambodia’s profusion of herbs. We take our name from the spice paste that has been at the heart of Cambodian cuisine for over one thousand years.Our founder and renowned Cambodian Chef Luu Meng has perfected the taste of the essential ‘kroeung’, which is lovingly prepared by hand using quality local aromatic spices at the peak of freshness.
